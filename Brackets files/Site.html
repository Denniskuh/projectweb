What are we about?

This is “The Wind Forum”. We’re a wind community which enjoys the life of surfing, windsurfing, kiting, … This forum is for all of you who’d like to share your experiences, your best gear and the best wind spots on the entire world. Share your sport, share your best stuff. LIVE, SHARE, LEARN on The Wind Forum.

Sports?

  • Windsurfing, the sport or pastime of riding on water on a sailboard while using the wind.
  • Kitesurfing, the sport of sailing standing up on a surfboard while being pulled along by a large kite
  • Sailing, the sport or activity of using boats with sails
  • Wavesurfing, Surfing is the sport of riding on the top of a wave while standing or lying on a special board
